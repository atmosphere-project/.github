### About ATMOSPHERE
**ATMOSPHERE** is a collaborative research initiative dedicated to forecasting epileptic seizures using physiological, behavioural, and contextual data.

Our mission is to develop **transparent, adaptive, and patient-centred AI models** that transform how epilepsy is monitored and managed — combining wearable data, self-reports, and explainable machine learning to provide early warnings and improve quality of life.
